About Us

Who we are

    
    
      

Galactic Company Core Division is an independent game development studio founded and owned by Mr. M. We are a solo studio with a singular vision: to build deep, narrative-driven worlds that players will remember long after the credits roll.

      

Our flagship creative universe is the Jack Bonecroft Universe — a sprawling, interconnected narrative spanning five distinct series, multiple dimensions, timelines, and generations of the Bonecroft lineage.

      

Every game we make is built with a focus on story, worldbuilding, and player-driven narrative. We believe that games are one of the most powerful storytelling mediums ever created.

      

Beyond the J.B. Universe, Galactic Company Core Division is developing additional projects that have yet to be revealed. Watch this space.
